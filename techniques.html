📚 Productivity Techniques

Explore proven techniques to boost your focus, beat procrastination, and work smarter every day.

🍅 Pomodoro Technique

Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer break.

Boosts deep focus and prevents burnout.

📊 Eisenhower Matrix

Categorize tasks as Urgent & Important, Important but not Urgent, etc. Prioritize wisely.

Helps manage priorities and avoid time-wasting.

⏳ Time Blocking

Schedule your day into fixed time slots for tasks, breaks, and learning.

Creates structure and minimizes multitasking.

🚦 2-Minute Rule

If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately — no delays.

Reduces mental clutter and quick task pileup.

💡 Eat That Frog

Start your day by doing the hardest, most important task first.

Beats procrastination and builds momentum.

📅 SMART Goals

Set goals that are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Realistic, and Timely.

Improves clarity and long-term motivation.
